## Selected Philosophy: **Neo-Brutalism meets Scientific Precision**

**Reasoning**:
This style perfectly aligns with Rongjian's background and goals:
1.  **"Scientific Precision"**: Reflects his background in Civil Engineering (Water Resources), Physics, and Quantitative Trading. It says "I care about data, logic, and systems."
2.  **"Neo-Brutalism"**: It's bold, stands out from the typical "clean corporate" portfolios, and leaves a **strong impression**. It signals "I am a builder/hacker," which fits the "Independent Developer" and "AI Infra" persona.
3.  **Differentiation**: In a sea of generic React portfolios, a raw, technical aesthetic signals deep technical competence and a "first principles" mindset.

**Implementation Details**:
- **Font**: Space Grotesk (Headings) + Inter (Body)
- **Colors**: Dark Mode (#0a0a0a) with Electric Blue (#3b82f6) and Signal Green (#10b981) accents.
- **Visuals**: Use "grid lines", "crosshairs", and "technical readouts" as decorative elements.
